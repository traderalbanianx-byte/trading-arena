Transparency First

Methodology

How we track, score, delay, and publish — and why.

01 — What counts as a trade

Any position posted in a trader's official Telegram channel counts — both spot and futures crypto, long and short. The trade is logged when the entry is published. Exit is logged when the trader confirms the close. Partial closes are tracked separately and labeled.

02 — The 48-hour delay

All data — new entries, updates to open trades, exits, and PnL — is held for exactly 48 hours before appearing on this website. The delay applies uniformly. Nothing is backdated. The timestamp shown is when the trade was originally posted, not when it appears here. This means what you see is real performance — just not actionable in real time.

03 — How monthly rankings are calculated

Rankings use closed, realized trades only. Unrealized PnL from open positions is never included in the score. The composite ranking score is weighted across four metrics:

04 — Four separate leaderboards

Spot and futures trades are never mixed in the same ranking. A trader who dominates futures but avoids spot would skew a combined board. The four boards are: Overall (all closed trades), Best Spot, Best Futures, and Best Risk-Adjusted. A trader can rank differently on each.

05 — Anti-gaming safeguards

  • All trades posted in official channels are logged — no selective inclusion
  • Losing trades cannot be deleted after logging
  • Partial wins are tracked at the target hit, not assumed at the full target
  • Open trade PnL is displayed but never counted in rankings
  • Records missing leverage or execution confirmation are flagged with a data quality note
  • Full audit trail is preserved — nothing is retroactively altered

06 — Data quality flags

Not every post includes leverage or a confirmed execution. When data is incomplete, the trade is still counted but marked with a quality flag visible on the record. We do not exclude trades due to missing fields — we flag them and include them.

Questions or disputes?

If you believe a trade has been logged incorrectly, the full Telegram post is the source of record. Every trade links back to its original message. Contact Dr Profit's team directly through the Arena community.
